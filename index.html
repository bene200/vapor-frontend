Annotation of Plant Genes based on Homologues in A. thaliana


This is a tool designed for the visualisation of genetic information about a sequence base on what's known about homologous sequences in a. thaliana.

We want to show how BioJS can be used on top of Galaxy to put relevant infomration on people's fingertips

As a usecase we focus on Arabidopsis due to the abundance of data on the organism. This allows us to show the power of the approach
